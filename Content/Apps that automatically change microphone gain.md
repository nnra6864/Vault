## Steam

1. Open `Settings` -> `Audio` -> `Advanced`.
2. Disable `Automatic volume/gain control`.

## Element

1. Open `Settings` -> `Voice & Video`.
2. Disable `Automatically adjust the microphone volume`.

## Discord

1. Open `Settings` -> `Voice & Video` -> `Show Advanced Voice Settings`.
2. Disable `Automatic Gain Control`.
