Mint, specifically wild mint, is commonly found in Serbian and Russian forests.
Alongside smelling good and helping with fresh breath, it's a mild topical analgesic and antispasmodic.

## Headache and muscle ache relief

Crush mint leaves into a paste and apply it to the skin.

## Stomach pain relief

Put mint leaves into some hot water and drink that.
