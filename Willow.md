## Headache relief

Salix species, which are common in both Serbia and Russia, are great pain relievers because they contain [[Salicylic Acid]], a precursor to [[Aspirin]].
To access it, find a young thing branch, about the thickness of a finger, slice it vertically and peel it.
Gather the green moist part from inside of the bark and either chew it to gather juices and spit the hard part out, or cook into a bitter tea.
As this is aspirin, you should avoid doing it because it can cause stomach irritation and [[Tinnitus]].
Start with 1 finger length piece and don't exceed 3 pieces a day.
It should start working withing 20-30 minutes, peaking at 1-2 hours.
